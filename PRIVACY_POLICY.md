# Privacy Policy for Hymns For Android

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in **Hymns For Android**.

### 1. No Data Collection
**Hymns For Android** does not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data. 

### 2. No Permissions Required
The app does not require or request any Android permissions to function. All features are available without needing access to your location, contacts, storage, or any other sensitive data.

### 3. No Third-Party Tracking
The app does not use any third-party services that collect information used to identify you. All hymn data and sheet music are stored within the application package and accessed locally without tracking.

### 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

### 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/lemuelinchrist/hymnsforandroid).
